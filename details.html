Wedding details

Ceremony & Reception

The wedding ceremony and reception will be hosted at Tyge William Cellars in Sonoma, California. We kindly ask the wedding is adults only.

Shuttle service

A shuttle service will provided between from the Courtyard Marriott in Novato and the Hawthorne Suites by Wydham in Napa Valley. If you choose to stay in other recommended accommodations, you may take an Uber or drive yourself. Uber service is reliable in the downtown Sonoma area. There will be an open bar, so please drive responsibly if you choose to do so.

Shuttle Service times will be updated here when available.

Attire

The ceremony and and reception will take place outdoors. We expect the weather to be 85 - 90° during the day and 50°- 55° during the evening. Guests will be walking on grass at the wedding, so please leave the stilettos at home. Don’t forget your sunglasses, and a shawl for the ladies as it may be cool in the evening.

Semi-formal attire is requested.
