Accommodations

We highly recommend booking accommodations as early as possible. Hotel prices can be very expensive in wine country, so please book early to avoid the high prices.

If you have any questions about accommodation options at anytime, feel free to reach us at laubidelman@gmail.com

Hotel Room Blocks

For guest convenience, we negotiated discounted rooms at two hotels. Shuttle service to and from the wedding will be provided from both hotels.

Hawthorne Suites by Wyndham in Napa Valley
314 Soscol Avenue, Napa, CA 94559

$197/night. 2 night minimum. Breakfast included.

To book the discounted rate, call 707-226-1878 and reference the "Lau Bidelman" wedding.

The Hawthorne Suites by Wyndham is centrally located in napa valley, with easy access to countless wineries. If you plan to visit Napa Valley during your stay, we recommend staying in this hotel.

  • ~23 min to Wedding Site (shuttle provided)
  • ~70 min to San Francisco (via Bay Bridge)
  • ~75 min to SFO Airport (via Bay Bridge)
Courtyard Marriott in Marin Sonoma
1400 N Hamilton Parkway Novato, CA 94949

$210/night. No minimum nights.

To book the discounted rate, call 415-883-8950 and reference the "Lau Bidelman" wedding.

The Courtyard Marriott is centrally located between San Francisco and the wedding venue. If you plan to primarily visit San Francisco during your stay, or only require one night of accommodation, we recommend staying in this hotel.

  • ~18 min to Wedding Site (shuttle provided)
  • ~41 min to San Francisco (via Golden Gate Bridge)
  • ~51 min to SFO Airport (via Golden Gate Bridge)
