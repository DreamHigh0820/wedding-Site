Things to do

The wedding is centrally located in Northern California, and a short drive from many world famous destinations:

  • 7 minute drive to downtown Sonoma, a historic town known for its charm, wine, and cheese. It's the perfect spot for delightful lunch or visiting boutiques.
  • 20 minute drive to the heart of Napa Valley, famous for its wineries growing arguably the best wine in the world. Don't leave without visiting at least one winery for a wine tasting experience.
  • 30 minute drive to the Golden Gate Bridge. Its red arches will connect you right into San Francisco, with countless activities and attractions for any type of person.
